Commands available:

artists
	Description: Lists your top 10 artists by song count in descending order

get info <artist>
	Description: Select an artist from your top 10 results to learn more information about them
	Will display the artist's name, a small summary, their years active, a link to their website, and a link to their wikipedia page

plot danceability
	Description: launches a plotly histogram for danceability audio features of your top 500 songs

plot acousticness
	Description: launches a plotly histogram for acousticness audio features of your top 500 songs

plot tempo
	Description: launches a plotly histogram for tempo audio features of your top 500 songs

plot speechiness
	Description: launches a plotly histogram for speechiness audio features of your top 500 songs
